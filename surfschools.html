Pozo winds is a multi discipline watersports center in the world famous windsurf spot Pozo Izquierdo. The center is perfectly located within a few meters walking distance from the main windsurfing spot. This center stocks all the latest brands and equipment, and all material is available to rent and buy. Pozo is better suited to intermediate and advanced riders due to the nature and condition of the spot. Pozo Winds offers rental and advanced lessons onsite at Pozo and for all other classes Pozo winds offers a mobile coaching experience for anyone wanting to learn or progress in their chosen spot. Beginner and intermediate windsurfing lessons are taught at another spot just a few minutes drive away where you'll find constant wind and flat water. If you would like more info on everything Pozo winds has to offer, please do not hestitate to checkout their website.

Oceanside is a surf school in Las Palmas Gran Canaria, This school is an accredited Roxy and Quicksilver school, who have been offering surfing to locals and tourists for 10 plus years. Oceanside surf school is located in Las Canteras and offers a few different options for enjoying your time in Gran Canaria surfing. Oceanside surf school provides rental, lessons and courses in Stand Up Paddle boarding and surfing for all ages, they also offer rental for surf skates and body boards. For more information on all the fantastic services oceanside has to offer, Please click through to their website.

Ipws is located in Pozo Izquierdo the world famous windsurf spot, The center is located just a short walk away from the waters edge making it nice and easy to access the water. Ipws is a multi discipline watersports center offering rental, course and lessons to all levels and abilities. Pozo is an intermediate to advanced windsurfing spot and not suitable for beginners. Ipws offers advanced windsurf lessons and rentals in Pozo and for all other levels, lessons are provided at another beach just a few minutes drive away. If you would like to learn more about what Ipws has to offer, checkout their website by clicking directly from this website.

Canary Wave is a surf school located in the south of Gran Canaria. Canary Wave surf school provides lessons and courses to all levels and abilities. During your lessons you will get to experience what it is like to be a canarian surfer and be taken to the best sandy beaches with the best waves for that day and the level of the group. If you're looking for accommodation and surf lessons then canary wave surf school can also offer this service, meaning you can surf in the day and relax and hang out with like minded people in the evening talking all about your greatest wave that day. For more information about this surf school click through to their website.

TWS (Tenerife windsurfing solution) is a windsurf dedicated center in El Medano and is just a few steps away from the water. TWS offer teaching and coaching sessions for all levels of windsurfers and at regular times throughout the year have guest pro coaches that deliver professional coaching sessions. If you have a little experience and consider yourself an intermediate to advanced windsurfer then TWS have a full range of equipment for you to test. TWS stocks all the top brands boards and sails and regularly renews the equipment so you are alway riding the most upto date gear. El Medano has a number of spots to suit all riding disciplines from flat freeride to wave conditions, For more information this or anything else about El Medano and TWS Please get in touch by visiting their website.

Surf Life surf school is located in the tourist town of Las Americas and is situated in walking distance from the main surf spots in the area. Surf Life surf school offers lessons and courses for all levels and abilities and teaches private lessons to children starting as young as 5 years old. If you are looking for an inclusive surf holiday including accommodation then Surf Life can arrange this for you offering a surf hostel experience which allows you to socialise with like minded people in the evening and enjoy surfing during the day. To learn more about this school click through to their website directly from here.

Secret Surf school is located on the east coast of Tenerife in an area called Poris de Abona and offers a wide range of surf lessons and courses to suit everyone. Secret surf school has a good selection of surfboards and offers a rental service from their shop for anyone who has a little bit of surfing experience. If you are looking for a surfing package holiday which includes all the material, accommodation and surfing lessons and you're looking to stay further away from the busy tourist areas then Secret Surf school would be a great option. To get in touch or just find more information about what Secret Surf has to offer, head over to their website by clicking the visit center button.

Flag Beach watersports is one of the oldest centers on Fuerteventura with over 35 years experience and is located to the north east of the island, just outside of the popular tourist resort of Corralejo. Flag Beach is a muliti discipline watersports center and is located directly on the beach a few hundred meters from the waters edge. Flag Beach watersports offer lessons, courses and rentals in all board sports and sailing for all levels and abilities. Conditions at this spot can be challenging and are best suited to intermediate and advanced riders. Flag Beach offers a mobile watersports experience for all their surf lessons and beginner, intermediate windsurfing lessons. Rescue is provide by Flag Beach watersports center and can be arrange at the centers reception.

Rapa Nui is a surf school located in the tourist resort of Costa Calma, Rapa Nui operates a mobile surfing experience picking their clients up from the accommodation and driving them to the best surf spots in the south of Fuerteventura for that day based on the conditions and group level. Rapa Nui also offers a surf camp and yoga retreat for anyone interested in a full surf lifestyle experience. You can find Rapa Nui in two locations in Costa Calma, at their shop in Costa Calma main street and also at their surf bar and restaurant which is located at the shopping center on the corner of the same street where the road leads down to the beach. If you would like more information about everything Rapa Nui offers checkout their website by clicking the visit center button.

Pro wind is located in Costa Calma in the south of Fuerteventura and is a dedicated windsurfing rental and lessons shop which operates a rent and go service. This service is first class and a perfect option for anyone wishing to freely travel around Fuerteventura and explore all the amazing windsurfing spots the island has to offer. Their lessons are tailor made to your windsurfing needs and can teach you at whichever spot is perfect for your ability or desired training. Pro wind have a wealth of experience windsurfing in Fuerteventura and are always willing to share their advice about the best spots and equipment for each and every day. These guys love windsurfing, so if you feel the same and want more information go checkout their website.

Nalu Surf is a Surf school in south fuerteventura based in the small coastal town of La Pared, Nalu surf school offers Courses, lessons and caters for all levels from beginner to intermediate. Nalu surf offers a mobile surf experience, picking clients up from their accommodation and taking them to surf on the south coast of Fuerteventura where the waves are best for that day. If you are looking to immerse yourself and experience real surf culture then Nalu surf offers a surf camp experience so that you can surf and relax with like minded people during your stay. For more information check out their website.
