Surf Schools Gran Tenerife

Below is a list of schools we thought could be useful.
If you can't find the information you are looking for here, simply click through to the schools website for more information

Franz surf school is located in Las America and is situated close to the main surf spots in the area. Franz surf school offers a number of different surf lesson options to best suit your needs and always teach in small groups so that everyone gets the best use of the lesson time. Franz surf school is not only dedicated to teaching you how to surf but they also want you to experience the wonderful world surrounding this sport. For more information about Franz surf school click through to their website.

Ocean life surf school is located in the tourism town of Las Americas where their shop is based a short distance from the water making it simple to get in the water and learn to surf. Ocean life surf school offers lessons and courses to a wide range of ages, levels and abilities. If you already have surfing knowledge and have surfed before and would just like to jump in and go surfing, then Ocean life surf school also offers a rental service from their shop in Las Americas. If you are a Canarian resident and want to learn to surf, don’t forget to check out the resident section on their website where you will find a number of discount codes for a slightly better rate. If you're interested in this or need a little bit more information then click through to their website.

Shaka Surf School is based in the tourism area of Las Americas, a short distance from the area's main surf spots. Shaka surf school offers surfing lessons to children and adults and is able to teach a wide range of levels and abilities. For those of you who already have some surfing experience then Shaka surf school has a good selection of surfboards to rent and will provide you with some local knowledge about where to surf and the conditions for that day. As Well as offering surf lessons they also offer a range of other activities to help you enjoy the water, including standup paddleboarding lessons and excursions as well as kayak excursions. If you are looking to combine surf equipment, lessons and accommodation why not get in touch with the Shaka team who will be more than happy to help you arrange your surfing vacation. For all of this and more check out their website with the link provided.
