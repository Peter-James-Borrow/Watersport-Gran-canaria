Surf Schools Fuerteventura

Fuerteventura is the second largest of the Spanish canary islands and is located in the Atlantic 100km off the north coast of Africa. Fuerteventura has a warm climate and fantastic watersports conditions all year round making it a great place to enjoy everything the ocean has to offer. Fuerteventura has a large number of watersports schools and rental stations scattered around the island which provide services for all levels, abilities and disciplines.

Below is a list of schools we thought could be useful.
If you can't find the information you are looking for here, simply click through to the schools website for more information.

Flag Beach watersports is one of the oldest centers on Fuerteventura with over 35 years experience and is located to the north east of the island, just outside of the popular tourist resort of Corralejo. Flag Beach is a muliti discipline watersports center and is located directly on the beach a few hundred meters from the waters edge. Flag Beach watersports offer lessons, courses and rentals in all board sports and sailing for all levels and abilities. Conditions at this spot can be challenging and are best suited to intermediate and advanced riders. Flag Beach offers a mobile watersports experience for all their surf lessons and beginner, intermediate windsurfing lessons. Rescue is provide by Flag Beach watersports center and can be arrange at the centers reception.

Point Break watersports is a multi discipline watersports school situated in the beautiful tourist town of Corralejo in the north of Fuerteventura, Their shop is located just off the main street close to kings surf shop. As Point breaks HQ is not located directly on the beach they offer a 100% mobile service, transferring and teaching their clients at a number of different locations around the north of Fuerteventura, and picking the spot where the condition are best suited for the day, this is a great way to experience the surrounding scenary Fuerteventura has to offer. To find out more about Point break watersports head over to their website for more details.

Home Grown is a surf school in the northern town of Corralejo which offers surfing lessons and courses to all its customers. Their main shop can be found in the center of Corralejo opposite the Shell gas station. Home Grown offers a mobile surf school, which can pick you up from your accommodation in Corralejo and take you to the best surf spots for that day based on the level of the group. Home grown also operate a surf camp where you can stay in their custom built surf hostel, socialise with like minded people and of course go surfing everyday. Work Sucks Go Surfing is thier moto, so if you like the sound of that and would like more details about Home Grown surf school then click visit center to learn more.

Ventura surf is a multi sport center, located in the stunning Corralejo bay area, The center has over 30 years experience operating in the canary islands. Their center is perfectly located and in easy walking distance from the main town and offers rental and lessons to suit everyone. The bay of Corralejo has some of the best and varied conditions in Fuerteventura making it a great spot for all riders. Beginners can enjoy the calm conditions in the bay protected by the harbour wall, whilst the more advanced riders can enjoy the more challenging conditions further out. A rescue service is provided by ventura surf at this spot. For more information press the visit center button to be redirected to the ventura surf website.

Lineup is a test center that offers lessons, courses and rentals in all board sports. Their shop is located on the outskirts of Corralejo not far from one of the island's top surf spots. Lineup operates a mobile watersports experience picking you up from your accommodation in Corralejo and taking you to the best locations and surf spots for that day. As Lineup is a test center they have some of the latest equipment available on the market which you can rent, try and even buy. To see more information about Lineup please visit their website.

Witchcraft is a small locally run custom board and sail brand that offers a rental and lessons service. Witchcraft boards are known as one of the strongest constructed boards available on the market. If you have a high windsurfing level and want to explore the north Shore of Fuerteventura then this is definitely the place to come to rent your material. The guys at Witchcraft have made the North Shore their testing playground and are always willing to offer advice on the best spots for the day. To learn more about Witchcraft, try some equipment,take a lesson or just get some insider spot knowledge checkout their website by clicking the visit center button.

ION Club Risco Del Paso is situated in a natural park 7 kilometers south of the tourist resort Costa Calma, ION Club offers a shuttle service between Costa Calma and the watersports center which makes enjoying this spot accessible to all. In my opinion Risco del Paso is one of the most beautiful spots to ride in Fuerteventura and is personally my favourite in the entire canary islands. The wind conditions can be challenging at this spot and a good level in your chosen sport is advised when heading out into the open sea, however don't let this put you off as Risco del Paso has a fantastic tidal lagoon which when full allows participation for all levels. ION Club is a multi-sport watersports center which offers courses, lessons and rentals. There are some restrictions on kite rentals at this spot due to the nature of the wind direction. This spot is very well lifeguarded and rescue is provided by ION Club with permanently staffed towers and jet skies. For more information on this amazing spot and what ION club has to offer, checkout their website.

ION Club Costa Calma is located in the south of Fuerteventura, The center can be found on the beach down the small path next to SBH Monica beach hotel. This center is predominantly a windsurfing center and offers courses, lessons and rentals. ION Club Costa Calma has a real family feeling to it and is a fantastic place for the entire family to learn and enjoy watersports. The spot where ION club Costa Calma is located, is best suited for beginners and intermediates as the wind and sea state are not too challenging. If you are a keen racer or enjoy slalom sailing then these conditions can also be found further out and away from the shore line. This spot is well life guarded and a rescue service is provided by ION club with staffed watch towers and jet skies during opening hours. For more information about this spot or what ION club Costa Calma has to offer please visit their website.

Matas Bay watersports center is located in matas blancas an area in the south of Fuerteventura just outside of the tourist resort of Costa Calma. Matas Bay is a multiple discipline watersports center offering courses, lessons and rentals. The wind direction at this spot is offshore and the seas state mega flat, making it an awesome spot to learn a new sport or work on a new maneuver or trick. This beach is lifeguarded and a rescue service is provided by Matas Bay watersports. If you would like more information about Matas Bay and everything they have to offer please checkout their website.

René Egli watersports center is located in the south of Fuerteventura and hosts the PWA world tour each year in July. The watersports center can be found on Sotavento beach beneath the Malia hotel. There are separate centers at this spot for Kitesurfing and Windsurfing, Kitesurfing to the right of the beach and windsurfing to the left as you look out to sea. René Egli watersports offers lessons, courses and rental to all ages, levels and abilities. The conditions at this spot can be challenging with strong offshore wind so a good level in your chosen sport is advised. This spot also has the benefit of a tidal lagoon which is ideal for teaching beginners and intermediates but can become crowded at peak times. René Egli watersport center operates a very effective rescue service and there are always lifeguards and jet skis whenever needed. It is best to check the conditions of the rescue on arrival as some rescues you may be charged for. For more information on this spot or René Egli services, please checkout their website.

Pro wind is located in Costa Calma in the south of Fuerteventura and is a dedicated windsurfing rental and lessons shop which operates a rent and go service. This service is first class and a perfect option for anyone wishing to freely travel around Fuerteventura and explore all the amazing windsurfing spots the island has to offer. Their lessons are tailor made to your windsurfing needs and can teach you at whichever spot is perfect for your ability or desired training. Pro wind have a wealth of experience windsurfing in Fuerteventura and are always willing to share their advice about the best spots and equipment for each and every day. These guys love windsurfing, so if you feel the same and want more information go checkout their website.

Rapa Nui is a surf school located in the tourist resort of Costa Calma, Rapa Nui operates a mobile surfing experience picking their clients up from the accommodation and driving them to the best surf spots in the south of Fuerteventura for that day based on the conditions and group level. Rapa Nui also offers a surf camp and yoga retreat for anyone interested in a full surf lifestyle experience. You can find Rapa Nui in two locations in Costa Calma, at their shop in Costa Calma main street and also at their surf bar and restaurant which is located at the shopping center on the corner of the same street where the road leads down to the beach. If you would like more information about everything Rapa Nui offers checkout their website by clicking the visit center button.

Nalu Surf is a Surf school in south fuerteventura based in the small coastal town of La Pared, Nalu surf school offers Courses, lessons and caters for all levels from beginner to intermediate. Nalu surf offers a mobile surf experience, picking clients up from their accommodation and taking them to surf on the south coast of Fuerteventura where the waves are best for that day. If you are looking to immerse yourself and experience real surf culture then Nalu surf offers a surf camp experience so that you can surf and relax with like minded people during your stay. For more information check out their website.
